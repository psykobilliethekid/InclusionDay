a Guide to Planning an Inclusion Project

This guide is to be used as a resource primarily for local education agencies(LEA's). You may envision investing in hosting an Inclusive Event. It is recomment that you take an integrated approach, taking advantage of the rich resources within your entire community. Any organization or agency your partner with could also use the information in this guide to host an Inclusive Event lasting a day or a week. You may wish to launch a series of events withing the school and broader community.
